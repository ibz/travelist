Frequently asked questions

Q: What is the ID of a trip?
A: The ID of a trip is a number that uniquely identifies the trip within backpacked.it. To find out what the ID of a trip is, go to the trip's page and look at your browser's address bar. It's the number you see just after "/trips/" and before the dash.
